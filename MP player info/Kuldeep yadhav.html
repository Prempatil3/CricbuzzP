Kuldeep Yadav


Kuldeep Yadav (born 14 December 1994) is an Indian international cricketer who plays for India and for Uttar Pradesh in domestic cricket. He was signed by the Kolkata Knight Riders for the 2014 Indian Premier League. He has played for the India Under-19 cricket team. On 18 December 2019, against the West Indies, he became the first bowler for India to take two hat-tricks in international cricket. On 17 January 2020, in the second ODI against Australia, Yadav became the fastest spin bowler for India, in terms of innings, to take 100 wickets in ODI cricket, in his 58th innings.
